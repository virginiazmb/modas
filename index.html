FLEUR HAUTE COUTURE 2022-2023.

A series of extraordinary evening gowns features abstract constructions, some with veritable bas-relief openwork bodices punctuated with illustrations by Pietro Ruffo. In this spirit, the Roman artist created a singular deck of cards in which characters disclose the graphic energy of the symbols. Dior gray appears in tweed, cashmere and organza on shirts, skirts, pants and capes. Meanwhile, the Bar jacket is revisited in black velvet, its curves reinterpreted to express a new attitude.

Splendid cards embellished in gold, enamel, and vegetal and geometric interlacing have a solemn and enigmatic presence, revealing an inner journey, like an adventure of selfdiscovery. A voyage to the heart of a castle populated by characters embodying the major arcana who question and disorient, inviting the viewer to look at the world from a new perspective.

THE DESIGNER

Creative Director and Founder, Paul Vasileff, learnt to sew from the age of three. Initially taught by his Grandmother he completed his first evening dress at age eleven, Vasileff was fascinated by the inner workings and underpinnings of garments. With dreams of one day becoming a couture designer, he developed his skills by undertaking private lessons in pattern making and sewing from the age of thirteen.

After debuting his first collection at age seventeen to raving reviews from the public and press, Vasileff immediately began taking orders for private clients. Wanting to further his skills and knowledge he sought out an apprenticeship with an Italian tailor practising in traditional sartorial methods, where he would develop his knowledge and technique. Shortly after, he was granted a scholarship to the prestigious Europeo Instituto di Design in Milan, Italy, where he graduated with honours. After returning to Australia, Vasileff expanded the business to employ and train staff that were equally passionate about honouring the traditions of fine sewing.

THE LABEL

The 2007 launch of the first Fleur collection marked the label’s foray into the fashion world. This original collection was designed, drafted, and hand-sewn by Paul Vasileff himself, at the age of seventeen.In the years since, the label has evolved to become one of Australia’s leading couture houses, specialising in garments designed to accentuate the beauty of the individual.

With a love of producing grand showcases, Fleur was honored to present in Paris for the first time in the 2016 with its Gilded Wings collection. A step in the right direction to achieving Vasileff’s dreams, Fleur has continued to return to the home of couture to showcase their work on the international stage. Often inspired by fables or fairy tales, Vasileff and his team create each collection holding true to the label’s mainstay. Key features of Paolo Sebastian creations include intricate hand-embroidery, elegant draping, delicate tulle, French lace, and romantic silhouettes.

ATEILER

Welcoming clients from around the world, the atelier tailors every element of the design process to the client. Private consultations and fittings combined with impeccable service are integral to creating a luxurious experience for Fleur clients.
Handmade in Adelaide, South Australia, each piece is brought to life through the mastery of Vasileff and his team of 18 specialised seamstresses. Having built a team that strives for excellence, each member exhibits the highest level of care and meticulous attention to detail that can be seen throughout every stage of their work.
